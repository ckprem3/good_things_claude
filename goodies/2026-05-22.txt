# Good Things: 2026-05-22

Four-Day Work Week Sticks: An Australian four-day work week trial published in Nature found almost half of participating firms saw increased productivity, and all but one company chose to keep the shorter week permanently after the trial ended. [https://www.positive.news/society/good-news-stories-from-week-21-of-2026/]

2. mRNA Cancer Vaccine Doubles Survival Time: A large multisite clinical trial of mRNA vaccines engineered to target cancer showed patient survival time doubled — from 6.7 months to 13.2 months — raising hopes for a new class of personalised cancer treatments. [https://www.goodnewsnetwork.org/]

3. China on Track for 17% Emissions Reduction: The New Energy Outlook 2026 found China is set to reduce greenhouse gas emissions by 17% this decade from their 2023 peak, far exceeding their own 2035 commitment, driven by record solar and clean-energy expansion. [https://www.globalgoodnews.com/]

Moringa Seeds Remove 98% of Microplastics from Water: Researchers confirmed that seeds from the "miracle" moringa tree reliably removed over 98% of microplastic particles from water, offering a low-cost, natural solution to one of the world's most widespread pollution problems. [https://www.goodgoodgood.co/articles/good-news-this-week-may-2-2026]
