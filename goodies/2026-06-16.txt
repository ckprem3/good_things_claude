# Good Things: 2026-06-16

1. Wind & Solar Overtake Gas Globally: For the first time ever, wind and solar combined generated 22% of global electricity in April 2026, surpassing natural gas at 20% — a landmark milestone in the clean energy transition. [https://www.euronews.com/2026/05/22/carbon-sucking-fungi-and-forever-chemical-crackdowns-positive-environmental-stories-from-2]

2. Kākāpō Breeding Record Broken: New Zealand's critically endangered kākāpō parrot (only ~235 adults surviving) completed its most successful breeding season ever in 2026, with over 100 chicks hatching and surpassing the previous record of 85 set in 2019. [https://earthlinged.substack.com/p/positive-things-are-already-happening]

3. Stanford Arthritis Breakthrough: Scientists at Stanford developed a treatment that blocks an aging-related protein, successfully restoring lost cartilage in old mice and offering a promising new path toward preventing arthritis in humans. [https://www.sciencedaily.com/news/health_medicine/]

4. Forever Chemicals Can Now Be Destroyed: Researchers discovered that hydrogen radicals generated by intense UV light can break down stubborn PFAS "forever chemicals" without any added chemicals, opening a door to cleaning up one of the most persistent environmental pollutants. [https://www.euronews.com/2026/05/22/carbon-sucking-fungi-and-forever-chemical-crackdowns-positive-environmental-stories-from-2]

5. Brazil's Atlantic Forest at Record-Low Deforestation: Brazil recorded just 8,658 hectares of deforestation in its Atlantic Forest in 2025 — the lowest level since monitoring began in 1985 and a 40% drop from the prior year, showing that conservation efforts are working. [https://earthlinged.substack.com/p/positive-things-are-already-happening]
