[https://www.sciencedaily.com/news/top/science/]

[https://earth.org/how-recent-conservation-wins-proved-nature-can-bounce-back/]

5. Green Sea Turtle Rebounds to "Least Concern": The green sea turtle was officially reclassified from threatened to "least concern" on the IUCN Red List, celebrating a remarkable recovery driven by decades of global conservation partnerships, protected nesting beaches, and fishing net reforms. [https://www.worldwildlife.org/news/stories/]
