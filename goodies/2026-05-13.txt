# Good Things: 2026-05-13

1. Scholarships Change Lives: Twenty young people who grew up as wards of the State earned their first university degrees, made possible by scholarships from the Supreme Ventures Foundation in Jamaica.

2. Teen Tackles Plastic Pollution: An 18-year-old innovator gained international attention for a creative new approach to rethinking and reducing plastic waste, offering hope for a cleaner planet.

3. Portugal Becomes Elephant Sanctuary: Portugal announced steps to become a refuge for elephants, giving rescued animals a safe and expansive home in Europe.

4. NASA's Psyche Spacecraft Swings Past Mars: The Psyche mission, en route to a metal-rich asteroid, performed a successful Mars flyby, using the planet's gravity to slingshot further into the solar system on its pioneering journey.

5. Coffee Linked to Lower Dementia Risk: A large long-term study found that drinking two to three cups of coffee a day is associated with a significantly lower risk of dementia, especially in people under 75, offering good news for coffee lovers worldwide.
