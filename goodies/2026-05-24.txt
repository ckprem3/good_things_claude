# Good Things: 2026-05-24

1. Jamaica's Murder Rate Drops 40%: Jamaica is celebrating a dramatic reduction in violent crime, with murders falling by around 40% in just one year, driven by a 94% increase in police tip-offs from citizens who are actively leading the change toward a safer country. [https://www.goodnewsnetwork.org/jamaica-celebrates-40-drop-in-murders-driven-by-94-increase-in-police-tip-offs/]

2. AI Detects Pancreatic Cancer 3 Years Early: Mayo Clinic's AI model (REDMOD) can identify signs of pancreatic cancer on routine CT scans up to three years before clinical diagnosis, nearly doubling detection rates compared to specialists working alone — a potential game-changer for one of the deadliest cancers. [https://newsnetwork.mayoclinic.org/discussion/mayo-clinic-ai-detects-pancreatic-cancer-up-to-3-years-before-diagnosis-in-landmark-validation-study/]

3. Virginia Becomes First Southern State with Paid Family Leave: Virginia enacted a paid family and medical leave program, becoming the 15th state nationwide and the first in the South to guarantee workers up to 12 weeks of paid leave for family, health, and caregiving needs. [https://virginiamercury.com/2026/05/12/virginia-becomes-first-southern-state-to-mandate-paid-family-and-medical-leave-for-workers/]

4. Sudan and South Sudan Eliminate Maternal and Neonatal Tetanus: Both nations achieved WHO-validated elimination of maternal and neonatal tetanus, with frontline health workers, midwives, and vaccinators delivering life-saving vaccines across all 188 districts — even amid conflict and displacement. [https://www.unicefusa.org/stories/maternal-and-neonatal-tetanus-eliminated-south-sudan-and-sudan]

5. NYC Creates 50 Car-Free Soccer Streets for World Cup: New York City Mayor Zohran Mamdani announced 50 car-free "soccer streets" near schools ahead of the FIFA World Cup, hosting youth clinics, art stations, and block parties so every kid in the city can experience the joy of the game. [https://www.goodgoodgood.co/articles/good-news-this-week-may-23-2026]
