# Good Things: 2026-06-27

1. Spiegel & Kerr Erase $550M in Medical Debt: Snapchat CEO Evan Spiegel and supermodel Miranda Kerr partnered with Undue Medical Debt to wipe out over $550 million in unpaid medical bills for more than 260,000 Californians — recipients need do nothing and will simply receive a letter saying their debt is gone. [https://www.goodgoodgood.co/articles/snapchat-ceo-miranda-kerr-medical-debt]

2. Record Leatherback Sea Turtle Nesting at Cape Hatteras: Four leatherback sea turtle nests were confirmed at Cape Hatteras National Seashore in 2026 — the highest annual total for the endangered species since monitoring began on those beaches in the 1980s, with 136 total sea turtle nests recorded this season. [https://islandfreepress.org/outer-banks-driving-on-the-beach/2026-becomes-record-year-for-leatherback-sea-turtle-nesting-at-cape-hatteras-national-seashore/]

3. Rachel Entrekin Makes Ultramarathon History: Rachel Entrekin became the first woman ever to win the Cocodona 250 outright, beating the entire field of elite men and women to set an overall course record of 56 hours and 9 minutes in one of North America's most grueling ultramarathons. [https://www.irunfar.com/2026-cocodona-250-mile-results]

4. Canadian Town Grants Rights to Trees: The Quebec town of Terrasse-Vaudreuil unanimously passed a resolution recognizing trees as living beings with "the right to life, natural growth, integrity and regeneration," becoming the first municipality in Canada to grant legal rights to trees, calling them "our biggest ally" against climate change. [https://www.cbc.ca/news/canada/montreal/terrasse-vaudreil-quebec-tree-rights-9.7243634]

5. Dr. Suzanne Huurman Makes World Cup History: Dr. Suzanne Huurman became the only female head of medical staff among the 48 teams at the 2026 FIFA Men's World Cup, representing Curaçao — the smallest nation ever to reach the tournament — and inspiring an all-female external medical team on the pitch for the first time in World Cup history. [https://gulfnews.com/sport/football/world-cup/meet-curacaos-suzanne-huurman-the-only-female-doctor-at-the-2026-world-cup-1.500582502]
